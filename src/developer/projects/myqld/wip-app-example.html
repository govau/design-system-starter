Services

  • Bond Loan

    A Bond Loan is an interest-free loan to cover the rental bond when you move into private rental accommodation.

  • Rental Grant

    A Rental Grant is a one-off grant of 2 weeks rent for people in a housing crisis. It helps pay for the cost of moving into private rental accommodation.

  • Bond Loan and Rental Grant

    Bond loan is an interest free loan to cover the rental Bond and Rental Grant is an amount equal to 2 weeks rent for the property you want to rent.
