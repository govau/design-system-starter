    Bond Loan and Rental Grant application

    A Bond Loan is an interest-free loan to cover your rental bond when moving into private rental accommodation. The loan is up to a maximum of 4 weeks rent, and must be repaid.

    A Rental Grant is a one-off grant of 2 weeks rent – it helps people in housing crisis to move into private rental housing. A Rental Grant is paid directly to your agent or lessor (landlord) after the tenancy starts.

    1. Check your eligibility

    You may be eligible to apply for a bond loan, rental grant or both, depending on your situation. There are different rules for bond loans and rental grants.

    Eligibility tool ( https://blarga.hpw.qld.gov.au/Eligibility/CheckEligibility ) Bond Loan calculator ( https://blarga.hpw.qld.gov.au/Eligibility/BondLoanCalculator )
