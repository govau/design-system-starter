Apply for a Bond Loan

A Bond Loan is an interest-free loan to cover the rental bond when you move into private rental accommodation.

Check your eligibility for a Bond Loan

To apply you will need:

  1. your contact details
  2. your household details
  3. evidence to verify your income
  4. evidence to verify your residency status
  5. your lease and property details
  6. details of your rental agent/lessor

Your application will take about 15 minutes to complete.
