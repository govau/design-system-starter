Austudy for students and apprentices

What is Austudy

Austudy is an income support payment for students and apprentices who are 25 or older.

You can’t get Austudy if you’ve already done a doctorate.

This is for:

  • Australian citizens, permanent visa holders, or protected Special Category via (SCV) holders
  • people with income and assets under a certain amount
  • people aged 25 or older who are enrolled in an approved course or Australian Apprenticeship.

If you’re unable to study full time you may still get a payment if:

  • you have a disability
  • you’re sick or injured
  • your course changes and you don’t have a choice to study full time.

How much you can get

The amount you get will depend on how much you earn, whether you’re partnered, or have children.

You'll get fortnightly payments for the minimum time it normally takes to complete your course as a full-time student.

Centrelink uses income tests to work out how much Austudy you can get. You can earn money while you get Austudy. The personal and partner income tests may affect how much you can get.

xxxx Your payment each fortnight includes Austudy and the Coronavirus Supplement. If you get $1 or more of Austudy, you’ll get the Coronavirus Supplement.

The supplement is $150 until 31 March 2021.

Check the maximum amount of Austudy you might be able to get.

Use Payment and Service Finder on the Services Australia website to find payments you may be eligible for and estimate how much you can get.

How to claim

Information you'll need to apply

  • Proof of your identity, such as your birth certificate, passport, or drivers licence
  • Your tax file number
  • Other supporting documents, depending on your situation

Claim online

To claim online, you need a myGov account linked to Centrelink.

If you don’t have a Centrelink account, you can establish your identity online through myGov and get a Centrelink Customer Reference Number (CRN). This will create a Centrelink online account linked to myGov.

After you submit your claim online, you’ll get a receipt and you can track its progress online.

If you can't claim online

If you can’t claim online, call the Students line on 132 490. 

How to report and manage your payment

If Centrelink approves your claim, there are things you must do to keep getting this payment. Go to the Services Australia website to read more about how to manage your payment.
